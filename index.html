01 — About

Designing with
purpose

I'm Sara Tamboli, a creative and detail-oriented UI/UX designer currently pursuing a Bachelor of Design in UI/UX at Vishwakarma University, Pune. I craft digital interfaces that put users first — beautiful, usable, and genuinely helpful.

My design process is rooted in empathy and research. I believe great design starts with understanding the people you're designing for — their goals, frustrations, and mental models. From there, I move through wireframes and prototypes to polished, pixel-precise visuals.

I'm passionate about solving real-world problems through thoughtful design — whether that's a mobile app, a dashboard, or a complete website redesign. Every project is an opportunity to make something that genuinely improves someone's day.
